Mad Libs of Oz

Pick a song title and create your own song from The Wizard of Oz!

A Mad Lib is an interactive story to which you add specific type of words when prompted.


For example, an ADJECTIVE describes something or somebody.
Lumpy, soft, ugly, messy, and short are adjectives.

An ADVERB tells how something is done. It modifies a verb and usually ends in "ly".

Modestly, stupidly, greedily, and carefully are adverbs.

A NOUN is the name of a person, place, or thing.
Sidewalk, umbrella, toy, bathtub, and nose are nouns.

A VERB is an action word. Run, pitch, jump, and swim are verbs.
Once all words are added, the story is ready. The end story is usually quite funny. Pick a song title below and insert your own words.
